About the UPSC CSE Exam

The Civil Services Examination (CSE) is one of India's most prestigious competitive exams. Understanding its design and scope is your first step towards success.

Plan of Examination

The UPSC CSE is structured in three stages: Prelims (objective testing for screening), Mains (written subjective papers evaluating depth of knowledge), and the Personality Test (interview validating candidate traits and decision-making clarity).
